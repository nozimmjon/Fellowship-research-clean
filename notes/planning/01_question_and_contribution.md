# 01 Question and Contribution

## Final Paper Question (Frozen)
"How persistent is educational attainment across generations in Uzbekistan, how has that persistence changed from 2010 to 2022-23, and which household and regional factors are associated with higher mobility?"

## Core Contribution
- Provide the first reproducible multi-wave LiTS evidence (2010, 2016, 2022-23) on intergenerational education mobility in Uzbekistan.
- Document trend changes in mobility with consistent measures across waves and policy-relevant subgroup splits.
- Deliver a transparent correlates framework (household and regional factors) without overstating causal identification.

## Scope Boundary
- Main paper: levels, trends, and correlates of mobility.
- Extension: pandemic-era mechanism evidence from LiTS IV child-learning module.
- Optional only: causal reform DiD, conditional on strong region-by-time treatment variation.

## Identification Language (Locked)
- Module A and Module B are descriptive/associational.
- Module C mechanism estimates are suggestive heterogeneity evidence, not standalone causal claims.
- Any causal language is conditional on passing design diagnostics and data-variation checks.

## Practical Payoff
- A finishable, publication-ready paper with one strong core contribution and one bounded extension.
